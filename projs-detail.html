SDGs Addressed: SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities)

Personal Initiative: As someone deeply connected to my African heritage, I felt a strong desire to preserve and promote the rich history and culture of Africa. This project was born out of a commitment to ensuring that future generations have access to their cultural roots.

Societal Impact: Wecaltural provides a platform for individuals to explore and learn about African culture and history, promoting cultural awareness and education. It not only educates users but also fosters a sense of pride and connection to African roots, making it a valuable resource for both local and global communities.

SDGs Addressed: SDG 2 (Zero Hunger) and SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being)

Personal Initiative: Driven by my passion for addressing global health challenges, I developed NourishKids to provide personalized nutritional support to children in vulnerable communities, ensuring they receive the care they need to thrive.

Societal Impact: NourishKids is an AI-integrated website that focuses on combating malnutrition in children under 5 years old.
