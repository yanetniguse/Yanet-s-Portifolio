NourishKids

Technology Used: Predictive Analysis (Python, TensorFlow), Behavior Analysis, Chatbot Integration, Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)

Background & Problem: Malnutrition affects millions of children globally, with many traditional solutions lacking the personalized approach needed to effectively address individual dietary needs.

SDGs Addressed: SDG 2 (Zero Hunger) and SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being)

Personal Initiative: Motivated by a passion for global health improvement, I developed NourishKids to tackle child malnutrition. While the predictive and behavior analysis features are still in progress, the integration of a chatbot provides immediate, tailored nutritional advice and support.

Societal Impact: NourishKids aims to provide customized nutritional guidance through a combination of predictive analysis, behavior analysis, and chatbot integration. Although still a model, the platform offers personalized meal plans and addresses dietary needs for children under 5 years old, contributing to child health and supporting SDG 2 (Zero Hunger).

Impact and Scalability: NourishKids is designed to transform child nutrition by offering:

  • Empowering parents with knowledge about balanced diets and essential nutrients.
  • Personalized meal planning based on predictive analysis, age, dietary needs, and preferences.
  • Healthy eating habits through accessible recipes and nutritional guides.
  • Solutions for common feeding challenges with behavior analysis.
  • Instant support and nutritional advice via the integrated chatbot.
The platform, still in its model phase, aims to scale and support more families worldwide by adapting to diverse cultural and dietary preferences.

Recognition: NourishKids has been recognized for its innovative approach, winning a hackathon and demonstrating its potential to improve child nutrition through advanced analytics and chatbot integration.
